Instructions on How to play BMCC-COMBAT
----------------------------------------
1. Pick a character from character list.
2. Re-name your character if you want.
3. Pick a weapon from the weapon list.
4. Pick an armor from the weapon list.
5. Start fight
   * You can use physical attack or magical attack.
   * Your get one attack per round. 
   * After you attack once, you have to allow your opponent to attack you. 
   * You can use health boosters to improve your health status. 
   * Each game only last 2 minutes.
   * You can quit the game anytime.
   * Game ends when either player's HP reach 0.
